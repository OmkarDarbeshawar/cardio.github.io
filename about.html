Title description, Sept 23, 2022

The Prediction of Cardiovascular Stroke is a classification problem. We must classify the provided data across multiple classes based on patterns in the input attributes in a classification issue. ”Stroke” and ”no stroke” are the two classifications available in our problem. We used multiple classification machine learning algorithms, as well as numerous sets of trials based on various feature extraction methodologies and algorithms, to categories this. We also spoke with medical professionals for data preparation to obtain precise details required for data cleansing. We implemented the algorithms after cleaning and preprocessing the data. We must perform hyperparameter optimization to improve the algorithm's efficiency. We used a combination of random search and grid search to fine-tune the algorithm, and we also used TPE (Tree Parzen Estimators) to verify the results.
